About me

Hello There!
I'm Nikolai - a 25 year old MultiMediaDesign student based in Odense. I'm currently at my third semester, and am now looking for an internship as a UI Developer / Designer.
Before starting my journey In the world of design, i was a sales advisor for a large shipping company, and then later at a mid level aftermarket company, handling clients in the heavy transport sector. Now every thing I do, is based around design and development, and i'm really enjoying the creativity surrounding both the development and design process.
I'm really looking forward to be getting even better at everything. Please continue reading and let me show of some of my skills!
